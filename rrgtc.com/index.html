The Red River Gorge Trail Crew is a community of volunteers.
Dedicated to providing access to the beautiful Red River Gorge through a well maintained trail system.

Outings take place on the second Saturday of every month.
Outings include clearing trails, building bridges, and other projects to help improve the Gorge.

The Red River Gorge Trail Crew loves new members.
New members are always welcomed and are invited to enjoy our volunteers only campground and potluck dinner after the outing.

To join the Red River Gorge Trail Crew, hop on over to the Join page. We are always looking for unique individuals to help maintain the Gorge and its trails.


All Red River Gorge Trail Crew service outings are official US Forest Service approved projects. All Red River Gorge Trail Crew participants sign on as official US Forest Service Volunteers.
